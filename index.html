About this directory

Québec Trade Directory lists skilled trades and residential or commercial contractors across Québec only—for example plumbers, electricians, roofers, HVAC, concrete, landscaping, renovations, and many specialty trades. Use the filters to narrow by trade category, city, or Québec region.

This page is intended to help homeowners and businesses find the right trade; always verify licences, insurance, and references before hiring. Sample listings here are for demonstration.

Star ratings and review counts are demo data. With a live site, collect reviews with moderation, owner replies, and spam filtering—then show aggregates only after you verify submissions.

Share filters: URLs update as you search (for example ?trade=Plumber&city=Montreal). Use Copy share link below to copy the current view. Favourites are saved only in this browser.

Licence requirements and trade names often differ in Québec (e.g. CCQ, RBQ, French language rules for signage and commercial communication). This demo is informational only—verify locally before hiring.
