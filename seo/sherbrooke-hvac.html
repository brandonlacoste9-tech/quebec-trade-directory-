HVAC & mechanical contractors in Sherbrooke & Estrie

Québec-only directory · sample data for demonstration

Efficient heating and cooling matter across Estrie’s climate swings—whether you’re maintaining a furnace, adding air conditioning, or sizing a heat pump for shoulder seasons. Good contractors document airflow paths, filtration, and maintenance plans—not only equipment model numbers.

Installation notes

  • Compare part/labour warranties and whether annual service is included or recommended
  • For electrification, discuss backup heat and realistic winter performance assumptions
  • Plan duct or distribution upgrades if you change heating type
