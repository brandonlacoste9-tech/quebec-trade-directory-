Plumbers in Montréal & Greater Montréal

Québec-only directory · sample data for demonstration

From duplexes and condos to light commercial, matching a qualified plumber matters for permits, insurance, and long-term performance. Greater Montréal mixes older housing stock, tower neighborhoods, and rapid infill—each with different access and code considerations.

Before you hire

  • Verify RBQ licence status, liability insurance, and relevant competencies for your project type
  • Ask how estimates, warranties, and emergency callouts are handled
  • For renovations, confirm coordination with other trades (e.g. electrical, structure)
